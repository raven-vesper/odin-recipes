really delicious bubblegum pizza recipe

this is a recipe for all the sweet and savory food enthusiasts out there! easy to make, easy to bake.

ingredients:

  • bubblegum
  • pizza dough
  • pizza sauce
  • parmesan cheese
  • italian seasoning
  • garlic
  • banana
  • blue cheese

steps:

  1. prepare pan. preheat oven to 350 degrees. spray a baking dish with cooking spray and set aside.
  2. mix together. in a medium-sized bowl, combine the bubblegum, pizza sauce, parmesan cheese, italian seasoning, garlic, and banana.
  3. add dough. spread the dough evenly on the baking dish.
  4. add the mixed ingredients from earlier.add the strange concoction onto the dough. be careful not to spill.
  5. bake to perfection!top with blue cheese and bake for 15-20 minutes or until the dough is cooked and the bubblegum is bubbly.
