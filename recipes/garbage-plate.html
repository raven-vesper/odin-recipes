the GARBAGE PLATE

this is any drunk college kid's best friend. it's literally trash.

ingredients:

  • wastewater
  • kitchen grease
  • batteries
  • pool chemicals
  • gasoline
  • bandages
  • needles
  • used medical masks
  • cardboard
  • chopped styrofoam packages
  • wet concrete
  • grass
  • twigs

steps:

  1. in a large skillet, fry the bandages and needles. add cardboard, stirring constantly with fork to keep its texture fine.
  2. once cardboard is cooked, add the wastewater and pool chemicals. cook for 10 minutes.
  3. then add twigs. cook 30 minutes, adding wastewater if needed.
  4. mix together the kitchen grease, gasoline, and used medical masks and place it into a large square foam container.
  5. fry chopped styrofoam packages until very crispy.
  6. cook wet concrete until well done and top with grass.
  7. place the we concrete burgers on top of the medical masks and fried chopped styrofoam packages.
  8. then, pour the cardboard with bandages and needles on top.
